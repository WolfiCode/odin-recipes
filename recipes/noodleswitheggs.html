Recipe for Noodles with eggs

Description

This recipe is the perfect solution if time is short. It contains a lot of proteins and you can easily add your own ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 250g of your favourite noodles
  • 3-4 eggs
  • 1/2 of an onion
  • 1/2 of paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Get the noodles ready
  2. Cut the paprika, onion or whatever you want to add
  3. Wait until the noodles are ready
  4. Put a pan on the stove and add some Oil
  5. Once it is hot, add the onions, paprika, etc
  6. Add the eggs and the noodles and stir
