Soda Lemon

Description

On a hot day nothing comes close to a ice cold, refreshing drink. Even better when it is healthy and contains a lot of vitamins.

Ingredients:

  • Lemon
  • Ice cubes
  • Sparkling water

Steps:

  1. Add ice cubes into a glas
  2. Cut open the lemon
  3. Squeeze some lemon drops into the glas
  4. Add sparkling water
  5. If you want you can add a slice of lemon to make it more appealing
  6. Enjoy!
